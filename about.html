About Us

Pach Fabricators is one of the leading and trustworthy fabrication firms located in Nairobi Embakasi area. We pride ourselves with over 15 years of experience in providing excellent, high-quality, and unique metal fabrication services and products to hundreds of business clients. With our passion and commitment to provide the best services, we have established strong relationships with our clients and closed more than 1000 construction projects. We are known for our high-quality products and services, as well as our commitment to customer satisfaction. Our team of skilled fabricators has the experience and expertise to handle various projects, including container fabrication, machinery fabrication, steel works, and much more.

Our Mission

Our mission is to satisfy our clients with our excellent, high-quality, and customized fabrication services and products using the latest technologies and techniques. We aim to deliver innovative solutions that exceed our clients' expectations while maintaining a commitment to safety, quality, and customer satisfaction. We strive to build long-term relationships with our clients by providing exceptional service and value.

Our Vision

The company's vision is to be the top choice in the market when it comes to metal fabrication. To be known for our commitment to quality, safety, and innovation. We aim to expand our services to other regions while maintaining our high standards of service delivery. We will continue to invest in technology and training to ensure our team is equipped to handle any project.

Mr. Erick Kirimi

Founder / Director

Pach Fabricators was established in 2007 by Mr. and Mrs. Kirimi, who envisioned a company dedicated to excellence in metal fabrication. With their leadership and commitment to quality, Pach Fabricators has grown into a trusted name in the industry, known for its precision, innovation, and reliable service.
